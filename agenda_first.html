In this opening keynote, a Cloudflare executive will unveil how the Connectivity Cloud empowers businesses to connect, protect, and build everywhere:

Connect - Regain visibility and control across on-prem, public cloud, SaaS, and the Internet.

Protect — Strengthen security while reducing attack surfaces, vendor complexity, and tool sprawl.

Build Everywhere — Accelerate innovation, automation, and modernization for agility and growth.

Join this session to gain real-world insights, groundbreaking innovations, and strategic takeaways to future-proof your digital ecosystem across ASEAN and beyond.

As Singapore advances its digital government and Smart Nation initiatives, the rapid convergence of platformization and AI is redefining how enterprises and public institutions approach cybersecurity. Traditional security models are being outpaced by the growing sophistication of cyber threats, requiring a fundamental shift in cybersecurity strategies to ensure digital trust, resilience, and regulatory compliance.

This session, led from a GovTech Singapore perspective, will explore how AI-driven security frameworks and platform-based architectures can enhance threat intelligence, automate risk mitigation, and fortify national cyber defenses. We will discuss:

How platformization enables a zero-trust, scalable, and integrated security approach across government and enterprise ecosystems.

The impact of AI on cyber risk, from adversarial AL threats to AL-powered defense mechanisms.

Real-world strategies for securing digital services, citizen data, and critical infrastructure in an AI-powered future. With cyber risks evolving at an unprecedented pace, now is the time to reset the cybersecurity playbook—leveraging AI, automation, and platform-driven security to build a resilient and future-proof cybersecurity framework for Singapore's digital economy.
